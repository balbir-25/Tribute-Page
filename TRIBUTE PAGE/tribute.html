DR. MANMOHAN SINGH

Manmohan Singh is an Indian politician, economist and statesman who was the 13th prime minister of India from 2004 to 2014.
The Man who changed Indian economy as Finance Minister in 1991

The Life Time Line Of Dr. Manmohan Singh

  • 1932 Born on 26 September 1932, in Gah, Punjab, British India,(now in pakistan) into a Sikh family.
  • 1958 Got married to Gurusharan Kaur
  • 1971 Worked as an economic advisor to the Indian Civil Services.
  • 1982 He was appointed as the Governor of Reserve Bank of India
  • 1985 Became the Deputy Chairman of the Planning Commission of India.
  • 1991 Manmohan Singh was appointed as the Finance Minister in the P.V. Narasimha Rao Government.
  • 1998 He was appointed as the opposition leader in the Rajya Sabha.
  • 2004 He was elected as the Prime Minister of India.
  • 2008 Manmohan Singh established the National Investigation Agency to fight terrorism
  • 2009 Manmohan Singh Re-elected as Prime Minister of India
